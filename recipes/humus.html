Humus!!!

Description

Prep:15 mins,Cook:15 mins

Servings:20,Yield:2,5 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 (19 ounce) can garbanzo beans, half the liquid reserved
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons lemon juice

Steps

  1. In a blender, chop garlic. Pour garbanzo beans into the blender, reserving about 1 tablespoon for garnish.
  2. Add reserved liquid, lemon juice, tahini, and salt to the blender. Blend until creamy and well mixed.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a medium serving bowl. Sprinkle with pepper and pour olive oil over the top.
  4. Garnish with reserved garbanzo beans.
